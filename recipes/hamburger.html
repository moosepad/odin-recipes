Hamburger

Description

Favourite food of a most of Earth's population

Not too difficult if this is your first attempt at cooking.

Ingredients:

  • Hamburger bun
  • Ground beef patty
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Fried onions
  • Slice of cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps:

  1. Place the lettuce and tomato on the bun
  2. Place cooked ground beef patty on the bun
  3. Add a generous amount of fried onions
  4. Add a slice of cheese
  5. Add preferred amount of Ketchup and Mustard
  6. Enjoy!
