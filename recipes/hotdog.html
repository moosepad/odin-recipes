Hot Dog

Description

Favourite food of a baseball spectators

Not too difficult if this is your first attempt at cooking.

Ingredients:

  • Hot dog bun
  • Hot dog sausage
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Fried onions
  • Sourkraut

Steps:

  1. Line the hot dog bun with condiments
  2. Place sausage in bun
  3. Add a generous amount of fried onions and sourkraut
  4. Enjoy!
