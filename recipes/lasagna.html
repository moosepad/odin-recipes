Lasagna

Description

Favourite food of a well known cartoon cat.

Not too difficult if this is your first attempt at cooking.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Bolognaise Sauce
  • spices

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees
  2. mix the milk, eggs and spices in a bowl
  3. cook the ground beef in a pan
  4. cook the pasta in boiling water
  5. add all the ingredients to a deep dish
  6. Sprinkle with cheese and place in the oven for 105 minutes
